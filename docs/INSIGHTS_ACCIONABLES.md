# Sales Insights

## Insight 1: Expand to More Cities

### What we found
- Santiago has only 3.6% of total sales
- There are 208 available cities but underutilized
- Most deliveries are local (no shipping cost)

### What to do
1. Focus on the top 20-30 cities with most potential
2. Create local warehouses in new regions
3. Offer promotions based on each region
4. Goal: Increase sales from other cities from 3.6% to 7% in 6 months

---

## Insight 2: Improve Discounts

### What we found
- Only 6 types of discounts in the entire business
- 20% of sales have no discount
- No variation based on customer type or quantity purchased
- Discounts are not optimized

### What to do
1. Create different discounts based on:
   - Customer type (Government, Wholesale, Retail)
   - Sale type (Physical Store, E-commerce)
   - Quantity purchased
2. Reduce unnecessary discounts without losing sales
3. Improve profit while maintaining volume
